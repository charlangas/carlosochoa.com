Medical-Rank.com

Experimenting with Programmatic SEO for Niche Lead Generation

We constructed and ranked thousands of AI-generated pages, dominating long-tail medical keywords through aggressive programmatic SEO.

Can you generate leads by ranking for thousands of low-volume, low-competition keywords? That was the premise behind Medical Rank, an experiment in programmatic SEO.

We built thousands of highly optimized pages, each targeting a unique long-tail keyword, with content generated by AI and a best-in-class internal linking strategy.

Despite launching on a new domain facing Google's sandbox, the site initially ranked for thousands of searches, achieving 13,000 monthly impressions and an impressive 20% conversion rate on page visits before being de-indexed.
