AI-Generated Websites for Financial Planners

Scaling Personalized Outreach with AI-Generated Landing Pages

We developed a Python and AI workflow to auto-generate hundreds of personalized, optimized landing pages for targeted B2B outreach.

Cold email alone often falls flat. How can we add instant value? For Rise and Rank, targeting financial advisors, I built a Python workflow that scraped prospects' existing websites.

It then used AI to generate improved, SEO-optimized copy and automatically populated conversion-focused templates, incorporating the prospect's branding, colors, and images.

This system created 600 unique landing pages in just hours. While results varied and the conversations weren't ideal, the positive response rate validated the approach.
